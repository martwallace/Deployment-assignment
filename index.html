Bio

U2 is a rock band that hails from Dublin, Ireland. The band was formed in 1976. Its current members are Paul Hewson (AKA Bono), David Evans (AKA the Edge), Adam Clayton, and Larry Mullen Jr. Their original name was Feedback (1976-1977) and then The Hype (1977-1978), until in 1978 they reinvented themselves as U2. Their career spans the better part of the last 4 decades. Their formative album, and what catapulted them to fame, was their 1988 album, Rattle and Hum.
